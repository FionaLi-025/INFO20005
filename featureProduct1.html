Description

Bearded irises are stunning flowering plants characterized by their large, showy blooms and distinctive fuzzy “beards” on the falls (lower petals). They come in a wide array of colors, including purple, blue, yellow, white, and pink, and are popular in gardens for their beauty and easy care.

Bearded Iris are ideal for providing tall accents in the garden bed and are the epitome of garden elegance. With their luxurious blooms and charming bearded petals, they’re the must-have statement piece for any Summer garden. Discover the beauty of bearded irises and elevate your outdoor space with nature’s own masterpiece. They are extremely hardy and are drought and frost resistant once established.
