Daffodils

Bring a burst of sunshine to your garden with daffodil bulbs from Garden Express Australia. Known for their iconic trumpet-shaped blooms and cheerful colours, they are a quintessential symbol of spring.

They thrive in full sun to partial shade and prefer well-drained soil. Their natural resistance to pests and minimal care requirements make them ideal for gardeners of all skill levels. From classic yellow varieties to vibrant multicoloured blooms, daffodils provide options for every taste and garden style.

Plant the bulbs in autumn and enjoy their radiant blooms as one of the first signs of spring.
