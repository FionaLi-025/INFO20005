Description

Showing off masses of pretty single flowers and a bushy upright growth habit, Fuchsia Bella Mariska (Fuchsia hybrid) is a compact growing variety that is perfect for putting on a long lasting display in a pot or hanging basket. The stunning flowers show off salmon pink sepals and a dark purple corolla, creating a dramatic contrast with the deep green foliage.

Fuchsias are a very rewarding plant to grow and the Bella series are no exception! With a compact growth habit and an early and prolific flowering habit, these shrubs will produce hundreds of delicately shaped flowers dancing all over the lush green foliage for most of the year.
